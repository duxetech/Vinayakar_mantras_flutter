App name: Vinayagar Mantras

Short description:
A compact collection of Vinayagar mantras with offline audio and Tamil TTS with word-by-word highlighting.

Full description:
Vinayagar Mantras provides a curated set of traditional Vinayagar chants and mantras in Tamil. Read chapters from bundled assets, listen to offline audio tracks, or use the Tamil TTS reader with live word highlighting for immersive learning and meditation.

Features:
- Offline audio playlist with next/prev and continuous play
- Chapter viewer with Tamil text from bundled assets
- Tamil TTS with sentence chunking and word-level highlighting
- About dialog and accessible controls
